COMMUNITY GUIDELINES
  • No bullying
  • Nobody above the age of 19 may join, though exceptions may be made in certain cases
  • Being respectful is compulsory, not a choice
  • When using dark humour, explicitely check in with the subject person
  • No bigotry (Including but not limited to racism, homophobia, and transphobia)
  • No disturbing content (Including but not limited to sexual content and gore)
  • It is requested and expected that venting be limited to only the Vent groupchats

    • Please help us in ensuring that this community is a safe and welcome space for everybody!

    COMMUNITY ACTIVITIES
    • SATURDAY GAME DAYS:- On Saturdays, those interested all vote on a game to play. For more information, please visit this webpage.
    • WEEKLY PROMPTS:- Every week, a prompt will be provided to interested members on which they must write a piece of literature (Eg: prose, short story, poem, etc.) For more information, please visit this webpage.
    • GENRE ROULETTE:- Every week, a wheel will be spun to decide a genre. Interested members must write a piece of literature (Eg: prose, short story, poem, etc.) For more information, please visit this webpage.
    • MONTHLY WRITING CONTESTS:- Every month, a selected range of genres along with a prompt will be provided. Interested members must write a piece of literature (Eg: prose, short story, poem, etc.) This is a competitive activity. For more information, please visit this webpage.
